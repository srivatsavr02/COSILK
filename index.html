Yarn section overview:

All our yarns are spinned from first grade DCH cotton in imported automised machinery to attain superior quality. The quality and counts are very strictly controlled by well trained professionals which is the secret for our success to be as a legend in yarn market. Prompt supply and reasonable rates with no compromise in quality adds value to our success.

Garments section overview:

All our garments are knitted with 100% combed cotton to suit for all exotic climate & to provide you good comfort and keep you cool. Its good sweat absorbing property always keeps you hygenic and free minded. The fablics are knitted with imported machineries and the processing are undergone in softflow units in eco-friendly zero discharge plants as per govt. regulations. Which give good bleaching and color fastness for all our products. The fabrics are manually checked and cut into pieces and then stiched with imported machines with high graded stiching yarns by well trained persons to give a perfect fit. They are inspected with quality controllers and packed to render defect free products to our valuable customers.
